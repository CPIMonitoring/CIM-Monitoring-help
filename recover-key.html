🔑 Recover Your License Key

Enter your Lemon Squeezy order number to retrieve your CIM license key.

💡 If you paid via card/checkout, your order number was in the receipt email Lemon Squeezy sent you. If you paid via UPI, your order ID was shown to you when your payment was approved (starts with "U").
Next steps:
  1. Open the CIM extension panel on your SAP CPI tenant
  2. Go to About → License Status
  3. Enter this license key, then click Activate
